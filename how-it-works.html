How TalkSyra Works

Simple Steps to Find Your Perfect Match

1

Download & Sign Up

Download TalkSyra from Play Store. Create an account using Google Sign-In or Email. Quick verification takes just 2 minutes!

2

Create Your Profile

Add beautiful photos, write about yourself, and set your interests. Your profile is your first impression!

3

Browse & Discover

Choose Grid View to see multiple profiles or Swipe View for a Tinder-like experience. Find people nearby or globally!

4

Like & Connect

See Live badges to know who's online. Swipe right to like or match with someone interesting!

5

Start Chatting

Chat with your matches using real-time messaging. Share photos, use typing indicators, and see read receipts!

6

Plan & Meet

Get to know each other better. Take it slow or plan a meeting. Always prioritize safety!

Safety Tips

Verify Profiles

Look for verified badges and check profile details carefully before connecting.

Never Share Sensitive Info

Don't share passwords, financial info, or personal details with strangers.

Meet in Public

Always meet your match in public places first. Tell a friend where you're going!

Use Block & Report

Encounter suspicious behavior? Block and report immediately. We take safety seriously!

Getting Started Questions

Is TalkSyra really free?

Yes! Basic features are completely free. Watch ads to unlock Premium features for 6 hours.

What does Premium unlock?

Global Mode (meet worldwide), Online-Only Filter, Verified-Only Filter, Typing Indicators, and Read Receipts.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed.

Can I use TalkSyra on web?

Currently available on mobile. Check our website for web app updates!
