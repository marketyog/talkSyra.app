TalkSyra Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to TalkSyra. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address and phone number
  • Age and date of birth
  • Profile photos and information
  • Location data (with permission)
  • Payment and billing information (if applicable)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information and identifiers
  • IP address and browser type
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Cookies and tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To provide and improve our services
  • To personalize your experience
  • To process transactions
  • To send you updates and notifications
  • To ensure safety and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no security system is completely secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account information confidential.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With your consent
  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform
  • When required by law
  • To protect rights and safety

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect functionality.

8. Age Restrictions

TalkSyra is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.
