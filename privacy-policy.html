Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 27 June 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to TalkSyra ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (TalkSyra).

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Application, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services, or otherwise when you contact us.

A. Personal Data

While using our Application, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including:

  • Name and Email: Collected via Google Sign-In or Email Authentication
  • Profile Information: Gender, Sexual Orientation, Birthday (to calculate age), and Bio
  • Media: Photos and albums you upload to your profile
  • Authentication Data: Provided by Supabase Auth

B. Precise Location Information

TalkSyra is a location-based discovery app. We collect and process your GPS location data to:

  • Show you nearby users
  • Calculate distances between you and other users
  • Provide location-based filters (e.g., "Same City")

Important: You can enable or disable location tracking in your device settings at any time. TalkSyra will not function without location permissions, but the choice remains with you.

C. Device and Usage Data

We collect information about your mobile device, including:

  • Device ID and unique device identifiers
  • Device model and operating system
  • App usage statistics (e.g., swipes, matches, and chat activity)
  • Crash reports and performance metrics

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process (via Supabase & Google)
  • To enable user-to-user discovery and matching based on location and preferences
  • To manage user profiles and display them to other users
  • To facilitate communication through our real-time chat feature
  • To provide Premium features (unlocked via rewarded advertisements)
  • To improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and protect users from harassment (Blocking/Reporting)
  • To personalize user experience and recommendations
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. DATA SHARING AND THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We share your data only with the following essential service providers:

  • Supabase: For secure database management, real-time chat, and authentication
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): To serve advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads
  • Firebase (Google): For sending push notifications regarding new messages and matches
  • Google Sign-In: For secure social authentication
Important: We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. ADVERTISEMENTS AND COOKIES

TalkSyra uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. These ads help keep the service free for users. AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as Android Advertising ID) to show relevant ads.

By using TalkSyra, you consent to the collection of data by Google for personalized advertising. You can manage your ad preferences in your device settings.

6. USER SAFETY AND MODERATION

Your safety is our priority. We provide tools for:

  • Reporting: Users can report profiles for inappropriate content or harassment
  • Blocking: Users can block any individual to prevent further interaction
  • Verification: We may offer verification badges to enhance community trust

We reserve the right to terminate accounts that violate our community standards. Our moderation team reviews reports and takes appropriate action to maintain a safe environment for all users.

7. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

We keep your personal information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy.

  • Account Deletion: You can request to delete your account at any time through the app settings
  • Data Removal: Upon deletion, your profile, photos, and chat history will be permanently removed from our active databases
  • Backup Data: Some data may be retained in backups for up to 90 days before being permanently destroyed

8. AGE RESTRICTION (18+)

Age Requirement: TalkSyra is strictly for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18 years of age. If we learn that personal information from a user under age 18 has been collected, we will deactivate the account immediately.

9. SECURITY OF YOUR INFORMATION

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures (including Supabase RLS policies and encryption) to help protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

We encourage you to:

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available
  • Never share your account credentials
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

10. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: You have the right to access your personal data
  • Correction: You can correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of marketing communications

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date and will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this privacy policy frequently to stay informed about how we protect your information.
